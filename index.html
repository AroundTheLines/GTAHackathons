Hackathons in the GTA

GTAHackathons is where you can find all the events to satisfy your hackathon needs,
right in the greater Toronto area.

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where participants can work on any software and hardware project of their choosing without distraction. At the end of the competition you will be given the chance to present your project to the audience for a chance at winning one of the various prizes. There will be mentors and volunteers to assist you at every step of the way.

But what if I'm inexperienced?

No worries! So long as you're willing to learn you'll be a great fit at a hackathon. They're great ways to get the experience that you're missing.

Why are you doing this?

Hackathons are amazing, and I want more people to experience them. They're places where ideas come to life, and you're missing out if you're not here.
